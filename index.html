3D Vision · Geometric Perception

Hi, I am Hanna.

I am a master's student in Electrical Engineering at KAIST, advised by Prof. Junmo Kim. My research focuses on 3D vision and geometric perception, particularly 3D reconstruction, visual SLAM, multi-view geometry, and autonomous driving.

I am broadly interested in reliable 3D scene understanding from visual inputs. My recent work explores how modern 3D reconstruction models can be made more consistent, scalable, and applicable to long real-world sequences.
